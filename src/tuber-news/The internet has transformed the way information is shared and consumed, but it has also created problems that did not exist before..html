The internet has transformed the way information is shared and consumed, but it has also created problems that did not exist before. What are the most serious problems associated with the internet and what solutions can you suggest?

Share this

The way of sharing and using the internet had been changing every year, Inspite of the advantages there are disadvantages too. The internet also causing troubles that did not happen before. The first half of the essay is going to discuss the problems caused by the internet and the second half of the essay is will provide the solutions for the problems related to the internet.

The the main problem associated with the internet is security issues. In other words, Confidential data that is stored or shared on the internet can be hacked easily and it is not a secure way to transfer the files. The chances of getting hacked through the internet is high. Cybercrimes are gradually increasing every year. It is very difficult to find cybercriminals. For instance, recently my friend's Facebook account got hacked. The hacker requested victims' relatives to send the amount via bitcoin.

There are some the solution to tackle these problems. To protect ourselves one must understand how cyber attack works and how hackers will steal data from us. Cybercriminals send the virus or worms or trojans with bat extension. This type of extension will get downloaded if anybody visit untrusted sites or when anybody download cracked software. In case, your desktop or laptop battery is draining quickly or your webcam light is turning on without your permission then you have got hacked. For example, I had downloaded a video editing software I had installed a cracked version of it after a few days I observed that my laptop is not performing well and the webcam light is turning on without my command. So, I restored my laptop by uninstalling that software.

In conclusion, the internet is a boon to mankind but some are using it in the wrong way and they are troubling others. I suggest that everyone should gain some knowledge about the internet before using it.
