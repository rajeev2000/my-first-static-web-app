More and more people are relying on private cars as their major means of transportation. Describe some of the problems associated with over-reliance on cars and suggest at least one possible solution. Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.


Usage of private automobiles are substantially increasing in these days. A number of individuals are choosing cars as their main mode of travelling. However, acquiring individual vehicles leads to many troubles, like transport congestion. This essay will discuss why this might be the case and how this problem can be alleviated.

The difficulties occurred by the utilization of own wagons are quite simple. Firstly, the road blocks have been the main trouble for every developing city and it has been a source of frustration. Moreover, preferring a wagon rather than utilizing public transportations like bus and train, will cause road blocks in many areas. For example, if every one owns a car then there will be heavy traffic problem that city might face. Therefore, government ought to find a solution for these troubles.

Hence, imposing of excessive taxes on private-owned automobiles can be the potential solution to this issues. This rule discourages the people to buy the vehicles, and the tax payer funds can be used to upgrade public facilities. For instance, this rule has been implemented in the United Kingdom, and they became very successful in controlling the traffic congestion. Hence, I suggest that every country need to adopt this rules on their policy.

In a nut shell, the private automobiles has become the major mean transportation, owing to the fact that it is more convenient and quick way to reach destination. Nevertheless, it brings problems in regard to the heavy transport issue. So, I think, applying the huge taxes on individual vehicles is the best solution. In fact, it has been proven as a successful fix in several cities like the United Kingdom, Singapore and etc.
