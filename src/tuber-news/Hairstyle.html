5 Men's Best Hairstyles

If you're looking to change up your man's look for a new season, consider getting a new haircut. Today's modern men's hairstyles have a broader range of options than ever before. Gone are the days of classically inspired cuts and slick backs. Instead, men are sporting more creative and daring looks that incorporate modern flairs. A classic style such as the fohawk is definitely out, and you'll have to go to a barber for a re-do if you choose to keep it. This hairstyle is too much for a man over thirty

For those with thick and wavy hair, you can try a fringed cut

It is a classic men's haircut that has a modern feel. For a retro-looking look, you can also try a low fade for a contemporary twist. The shorter sides of this hairstyle will accentuate the slick back style of the face. The short side swept style is great for men with straight to medium-length hair.

Highlighted fringe has been a popular hairstyle for years

It was probably trademarked by Nate Archibald from the TV show Gossip Girl. The spiky hairstyle is great for preppy guys, triangular faces, and thick hair. Moreover, it goes well with a wide neck and a comb-over. In addition, it looks great with any type of outfit.

A slick back is another classic men's hairstyle

It's easy to create and maintain and is very versatile. There are many different styles to choose from. To find the right one for your man, check out some pictures of the different styles. It'll help you decide if it's right for him or not. It's always good to try a few before making a final decision.

The highlight fringe is a timeless style for men

The modern version of this hairstyle is perfect for square, oval, or triangular faces. The fringe is also a great choice for men with thick or coarse hair. This classic style is perfect for any preppy guy with a spiky beard. And if you're in doubt, it's worth trying it.

The crispy fringe has long been a popular men's haircut for many years

This spiky cut is an updated version of the classic Caesar cut. The crispy fringe is a shaved line that extends from the neckline to the temple. This hairstyle is perfect for men with thick or coarse hair, and it's ideal for all shapes and sizes. For the most stylish guys, a slick fringe is the best option.
