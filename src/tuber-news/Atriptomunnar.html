Best places to visit in munnar.

01 Munnar top station place.
This article is a guide to must visit places in munnar.

Rested at a height of 1700 m and overlooking the awesome panoramic views of Ghats is the hill station in Munnar — one of the most attractive places to visit in Munnar in a single day. Feel the whisper of cool breeze in your face and watch the Theni valley stretched out in front of you. An ideal vantage point to view tiny human settlements peppered near the Kerala-Tamil Nadu border, the top station will make you feel like you are on the peak of the world.

02 Tea Estate.

As soon you step into the estates, you will be filled with the perfume of tea plants, garden-fresh tea leaves being picked, and tea is processed. Housing more than fifty tea estates, going to one of these properties is a must when in Munnar. Rich green tea plants are spread over as far as the eye can see and provide you a sense of calmness and peace that only comes with spending time around nature.

03 Lakkam Waterfalls.

One of the most interesting places to visit in Munnar in one day is Lakkam waterfalls, one of the nearest waterfalls in the area. Originating from Eravikulam Plateau and tucked away on the Munnar-Marayoor route, visiting this waterfall will be a memory you will cherish your entire life. Watch the stream create inaccessible cascades of water as it flows down from the plateau. Popular amongst families and honeymooners, the sight at Lakkam will be a visual treat for all those who take off the journey to visit it.
